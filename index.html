FAQ

Frequently asked

What's the Design Agency delivery process?

The Design Agency process is straight forward and collaborative. It starts with a kickoff call to discuss your goals and inspirations. You provide feedback as we manage the rest. We keep you updated every two days via written messages or Loom videos.

What if I'm not satisfied with my designs?

Feel free to send it back to us for revisions; we'll keep refining it until it meets your expectations.

Is there a limit to the number of requests I can make?

Nope, there's no limit to your requests! We'll tackle them one by one until everything is completed and approved. For A La Carte customers, we suggest keeping a few items on your TODO list to keep our team busy.

Why not just hire a full-time designer?

Hiring a full-time designer can be costly and challenging to manage. A senior designer can easily exceed $100K annually, plus benefits and vacation days. It adds up quickly!

How do I share feedback?

We value customer feedback! You can share your thoughts in three main ways: via a Loom video, Figma comments, or a message. While we prefer Loom videos or Figma comments, any combination of these methods works perfectly!

What's your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your subscription anytime with a written notice at least 18 days before your next billing date. If you cancel within 18 days of renewal, your account will renew for one more month, and cancellation will take effect at the end of that month.

What is Dual Nova Lab's development process?

Our development process is collaborative and transparent. We begin with a discovery phase to understand your requirements, followed by planning, design, development, testing, and deployment. We provide regular updates throughout the project lifecycle.

What technologies does Dual Nova Lab specialize in?

We specialize in a wide range of technologies including React, Angular, Node.js, Python, Flutter, React Native, AWS, Google Cloud, and various database solutions. Our tech stack is chosen based on your specific project requirements.

How do you ensure the quality of your deliverables?

We implement rigorous quality assurance processes including automated testing, code reviews, and performance optimization. Our development follows industry best practices to ensure scalable, secure, and maintainable solutions.
